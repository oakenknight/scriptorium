# Annals

> Chronological records: meeting logs, journal entries, daily notes,
> and weekly reviews. Everything here is anchored to a date. Records
> are created and maintained by the Chronicler. The Custodian creates
> year subfolders when they are first needed.

## Notes

- Records are organized in `Annals/<YYYY>/` subfolders
- Meeting logs: `YYYY-MM-DD.meeting.md`
- Journal entries: `YYYY-MM-DD.journal.md`
- Daily notes: `YYYY-MM-DD.daily.md`
- Weekly reviews: `YYYY-WXX.review.md`
- Year indexes (`Annals/<YYYY>/_index.md`) are maintained by the
  Chronicler
- The Custodian creates year folders; the Chronicler creates notes
  within them
