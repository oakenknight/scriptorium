# Reliquary

> Archive: completed projects, dormant work, and historical records.
> Nothing is deleted — it is moved here. The Reliquary is organized by
> year. The Custodian moves projects here when they close. The Oracle
> can retrieve from the Reliquary; it is searchable.

## Notes

- Archived projects live in `Reliquary/<YYYY>/<project-slug>/`
- Year folders and their indexes are created by the Custodian
- Notes in the Reliquary are read-only — do not edit archived records
- If a project is reactivated, the Custodian moves it back to
  `Compendium/`
- The Archivist may audit the Reliquary for completeness but does not
  modify it
