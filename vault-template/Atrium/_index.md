# Atrium

> Raw input: everything lands here first. Notes in the Atrium are
> unprocessed. They are not permanent records. The Quaestor routes
> them to their correct home; the Chronicler formats meeting content;
> the Intake creates them. Nothing lives in the Atrium permanently.
